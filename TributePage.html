Late. Sushant Singh Rajput

Sushant Singh Rajput was an allrounder. He performed exceptionally in all the fields he laid his hand on. He was a very studious student right from his early age of schooling, he excelled in all his classes. He prepared for engineering entrance exam and cleared it with flying colours, he secured ALL INDIA Rank 7. He took admission in Delhi Technological University and pursued his bachelor of engineering in Mechanical engineering. He had keen interest in dancing and acting, later he dropped out from college with a dream of becoming an actor. He shifted to Mumbai from Delhi with dreams in his eyes. He struggled a lot in his early days living in Mumbai, but later with his hardwork and gods blessing he debuted in Star Plus's romantic drama Kis Desh Mein Hai Meraa Dil in 2008 later he got starring role in Zee TV's daily soap opera Pavitra Rishta, he also worked in some reality shows but Pavitra Rishta gave him a thrust which he required in rising to stardom and then finally he got his first film Kai Po Che!..

Later he gave many big hits ike - -

  • MS Dhoni: The Untold Story
  • Chhichhore
  • Shuddh Desi Romance
  • Drive

Last film he shot was Dil Bechara but is not released yet, Its trailer got out few days back and his fans supported it too much making it most liked trailer on YouTube with 10 million + likes and are eagerly waiting for the movie.

He had keen interest in space and also bought a plot on moon, he also had an advance microscope and used to watch activities beyond earth.

On the day of 14th June 2020 he hanged himself in his room, it seems like a suicide but his close ones are claiming it to be a murder. Many bollywood actors stood up for him and said that he was depressed , and the reason is supposedly nepotistm. He was banned by many big production houses, he never got praised for his exemplary work he portrayed on screen, He was always ignored by other actors because he was not a starkid. Its a bitter truth that nepotism took away a brilliant actor and future superstar from us. Many people paid condolences on his death.

There is still a debate all over the India that if its a murder or suicide and many big faces in entertainment industry and political figures are demanding CBI enquiry in this case. But whatsoever it was we can't get back our star back.Let's pray together his soul may rest in peace in heaven.
